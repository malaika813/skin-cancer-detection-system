Skin Cancer Resources

Symptoms of Skin Cancer

  • New or unusual growths or moles
  • Asymmetrical moles or lesions
  • Changes in color, size, or shape of a mole
  • Bleeding or crusting moles
  • Lesions that don’t heal

Precautions

  • Use sunscreen daily (SPF 30+)
  • Avoid tanning beds and prolonged sun exposure
  • Wear protective clothing, sunglasses, and hats
  • Stay in the shade during peak sun hours (10am–4pm)
  • Check your skin monthly for any changes

Tips

  • Visit a dermatologist annually for skin checkups
  • Keep a record of your moles using photos
  • Teach your children about sun safety early
  • Use mobile apps or tools (like this AI system) for early detection
